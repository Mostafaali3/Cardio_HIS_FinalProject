Our services

1-Diagnostic Survices

We can provide diagnosis using Different technologies:

-Electrocardiogram (ECG): Measures the electrical activity of the heart to detect abnormalities.

-Cardiac MRI and CT Scans: Advanced imaging techniques to obtain detailed pictures of the heart and its structures.

-Coronary Angiography: Uses X-ray imaging to see the heart’s blood vessels and identify blockages.

2-Treatment Survices

We can provide you Different treatment methods:

-Medications: Prescribing drugs to manage conditions such as hypertension, arrhythmias, heart failure, and high cholesterol.

-Heart Failure Management: Comprehensive care plans for patients with heart failure, including medication management, lifestyle changes, and sometimes device implantation.

3-Preventive Care

Our Preventive Services:

-Risk Factor Assessment: Evaluating and managing risk factors like high blood pressure, high cholesterol, diabetes, and smoking.

-Lifestyle Counseling: Advice on diet, exercise, and other lifestyle changes to improve heart health.

-Cardiac Rehabilitation: A structured program of exercise, education, and counseling to help patients recover from heart-related issues and prevent future problems.

4-Surgeries

Cardiac Surgeries Which Includes:

-Coronary Artery Bypass Grafting (CABG): Surgery to improve blood flow to the heart.

-Valve Repair or Replacement: Surgery to fix or replace damaged heart valves.

-Pacemaker and Implantable Cardioverter-Defibrillator (ICD) Implantation: Devices to manage heart rhythms.

5-Emergencies

Emergency Services:

-Emergency Cardiac Care: Immediate treatment for acute conditions like heart attacks, including thrombolysis (clot-busting drugs) and emergency angioplasty.

-Cardiovascular Intensive Care Unit (CICU): Specialized care unit for critically ill cardiac patients.
